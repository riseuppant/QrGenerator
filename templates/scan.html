How to Use QR Attendance Tracker

Follow these simple steps to track attendance:

  1. Go to the Scan tab and select your event
  2. Click "Start Tracking Attendance" to initialize the scanner
  3. Click "Start Camera" to begin scanning QR codes
  4. Scan student QR codes to mark attendance
  5. View results in the "Results" tab
  6. Generate reports in the "Reports" tab
  7. Export data in various formats from the "Export" tab

Troubleshooting

  • If camera doesn't start, try refreshing the page or switch cameras
  • For best results, ensure good lighting and a steady hand
  • QR codes should be displayed clearly and not damaged
